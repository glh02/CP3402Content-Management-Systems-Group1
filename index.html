About Us

Founded in 2013, Baizonn Learning Center believes in establishing a Strong Foundation built on grasping Maths and Science principles. Our F.A.S.T. approach (Firm Foundation, Apply, Surpass, Top) empowers students from Primary to Junior College to excel.

Our Programs

  • Primary School: Mathematics, Science, PSLE Prep
  • Secondary School: Sec 1-4 Maths & Science, GCE 'O' Levels
  • Junior College: GCE 'A' Levels Maths & Science
